Amazon Review Sentiment Analysis

Welcome to the Amazon Review Sentiment Analysis application!

To use this app, simply enter your product review in the text box on the next page. Here are some guidelines to help you input the data correctly:

  • Write a detailed review of the product, similar to reviews you see on e-commerce websites.
  • Focus on your experience with the product, including any features you liked or disliked.
  • Do not include numbers or special characters in your review. Use only letters and spaces.
  • Here are some examples of invalid reviews:
    • "Great product! 100% satisfied!! #awesome"
    • "The price was $299.99 but totally worth it!!"
    • "Loved it!!! <3 <3 <3"
  • Here are some examples of valid reviews:
    • "The phone never skips a beat. File transfers are speedy with no corruption issues."
    • "Purchased this device and it worked as advertised. Never had much phone memory before but this is a no-brainer."
    • "Works as expected. Higher capacity and seems well-made. Paint looks clean."

After you submit your review, the application will analyze the sentiment and display whether your review is positive, negative, or neutral.
